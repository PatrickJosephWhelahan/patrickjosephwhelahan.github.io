Patrick Joseph, known as Joe is a Product Designer based in Dublin, Ireland. Joe currently works at IBM Design where he is responsible for all phases of the UX process, from research, concepting and information architecture to wireframing, prototyping, storytelling, and facilitation of Enterprise Design Thinking.

At IBM he collaborates with fellow designers, developers and product owners to create products and experiences for a wide range of clients.



Joe lectures at Dublin City University specilaising in UX and UI module delivery for under- and post-grad students.

He received a BSc (1st Class Honours) in Digital Technology Design and Innovation from Dublin Institute of Tecnholgy in 2015.
